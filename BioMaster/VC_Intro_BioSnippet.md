# VC Intro – Bio Snippet (Short Form)

**English (primary for decks / email)**

> *“I’m not trying to build the next big platform. I’m building the field OS that all platforms will eventually need.”*  
> — Phillip Mendoza

Phillip Mendoza (“Lung”) is a field‑first system architect behind **Reflex801 / VoiceGold**, a local operating schema that turns real voices from Thai SMEs into structured, auditable data assets for AI systems, investors, and policymakers.

- 15+ years spanning creative, factory‑side production, and SME fieldwork in Thailand  
- Architect of **Human Land (HL) / Land Lens (LL)** – a dual‑layer schema for capturing and interpreting ground‑truth data
- Builder of **VoiceGold / GENVOZ**, modules that transform raw field audio into forward‑looking economic signals
- Currently preparing Reflex801 / VoiceGold for external review by global labs and foundations focused on *ground‑truth AI* and data ethics

Mendoza’s work does not compete with model labs; it **feeds** them.  
Reflex801 is designed to sit underneath any AI stack as a human‑anchored data spine – starting with Thai SMEs, but generalizable to other emerging markets.
