ระบบที่ใช้เป็นแกนของงานทั้งหมดคือ **Reflex801 / VoiceGold** – โครงสร้างคิดและโครงสร้างเก็บข้อมูลที่ออกแบบมาเพื่อยืนยันว่า  
> ถ้าเรารับฟังคนตัวเล็กอย่างจริงจัง แล้วเก็บข้อมูลอย่างมีวินัย เสียงพวกนี้จะกลายเป็นสินทรัพย์ข้อมูลระดับประเทศได้

## EN – English Version

Phillip Mendoza, known as “Lung”, is a field‑first system builder who stands between **Thai micro‑entrepreneurs**, **real‑world data**, and **large‑scale AI ecosystems**.  
He does not position himself as a pure technologist, but as someone who walks markets, talks to vendors, records their voices and stories, and then turns those signals into structured data that AI systems can work with *without erasing the people behind them*.

The backbone of his work is **Reflex801 / VoiceGold** – a design schema and data OS built to demonstrate that:

> If we listen carefully to small voices and capture them with discipline, those voices can become a national‑level data asset.

### Core Roles

- **Custodian** – guarding ethics, consent, and dignity across every field case that enters the system.
- **Field System Architect** – designing capture flows SMEs can use with everyday tools (Drive, GitHub, Sheets, phones).
- **Human–AI Translator** – turning lived experience and speech into structured inputs that models across vendors can read and reason over.

Reflex801 / VoiceGold is not a giant platform; it is a **field OS** that others can learn from, fork, and adapt – for both humans and AI agents that want to stay grounded in reality.
